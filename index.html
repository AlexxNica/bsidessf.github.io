BSidesSF 2017

We are excited to announce that BSidesSF will take place February 12th and 13th, 2017 at DNA Lounge and BuzzWorks. This year's theme features everyone's favorite hacker TV series, Mr. Robot. Tickets as well as goodies like electornic badges and t-shirts are now on sale on the ticket page.

BSidesSF wants to give a big shout out to everyone who contributed to the 112 CFP submissions we recieved! You can see the first round of selections here and can check back for the second round of selections soon.

We look forward to seeing you this coming February. If you don't find the information you are looking for on this site don't hesitate to reach out to info [at] bsidessf.com.
